Cannabis Trade Fairs, Festivals and Events in Czech Republic

Published October 16, 2025 | Reading time: 6 minutes

The Czech Republic is home to several significant cannabis trade fairs and festivals that attract thousands of visitors from around the world. These events are a great opportunity to learn about industry innovations, purchase products, and meet the community.

Major Cannabis Events

Here's an overview of the most important cannabis trade fairs and festivals you shouldn't miss.

Event Name Date Location Description
Cannafest November 7-9, 2025 PVA Expo Prague The world's largest international cannabis trade fair. The 15th edition offers a showcase of growing equipment, fertilizers, seeds, smoking accessories and vaporizers [1].
Growfest March 27-29, 2026 Brno Exhibition Center, Pavilion E A trade fair focused on cultivation and everything related to it. The celebration of World Cannabis Day takes place here on April 20 [2].
Konopex April 24-26, 2026 Trojhal√≠ Karolina, Ostrava The 9th edition of the cannabis festival and trade fair in Ostrava, featuring Czech and international exhibitors and a rich accompanying program [3].

Social Clubs and Lounges

Although Prague doesn't have official cannabis social clubs like in Spain, there are establishments that function as "cannabis-friendly" lounges where consumption is tolerated.

  • Kingstown Cannabis Lounge (Prague): This establishment profiles itself as "the best coffeeshop in Prague" and is known for its friendly atmosphere for cannabis smokers.
  • Esperar Cannabis Social Club CZ (Prague): An association working to create a full-fledged social club. Follow their activities for future developments.
