Published October 16, 2025 | Reading time: 7 minutes

Remember the Laws and Etiquette

Even in the friendliest establishments, it's important to follow several rules:

  • Don't smoke indoors: The smoking ban applies everywhere. Always look for outdoor spaces.
  • Be discreet: Even on the patio, be considerate of other guests.
  • Support the establishment: Always order drinks or food. These bars are not public smoking lounges.
  • Don't sell or buy: These establishments tolerate personal consumption, not commerce.
