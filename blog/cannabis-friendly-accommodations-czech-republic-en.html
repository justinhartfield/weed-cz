Cannabis-Friendly Accommodations in Czech Republic

Published October 16, 2025 | Reading time: 8 minutes

The Czech Republic, especially Prague, is becoming an increasingly popular destination for cannabis tourists. Although you won't find Amsterdam-style coffeeshops where THC is legally sold, the approach to cannabis here is generally relaxed. This guide will help you find the best accommodations where cannabis consumption is tolerated or even welcomed.

Hotels and Hostels in Prague

Prague offers several options for smokers, from luxury hotels to specialized hostels that cater to 420-friendly clientele.

Luxury Options

  • The Alcron Hotel: This five-star Art Nouveau-style hotel offers not only luxury accommodation but also a modern approach to cannabis. Some sources indicate that the hotel offers special packages combining beer and cannabis tasting [1].
  • Hotels with smoking rooms: Many Prague hotels still offer smoking rooms. These include Botanique Hotel Prague, Hilton Prague, and Hotel Opera. However, it's always necessary to verify in advance whether cannabis smoking is permitted in these rooms.

Specialized Hostels

  • Prague City Canna Hostel: As the name suggests, this hostel is aimed directly at cannabis enthusiasts. Located in the center of Prague, it offers a wide range of rooms. According to available information, cannabis consumption is encouraged both indoors and outdoors [2].
"Yes, some Airbnb hosts and boutique hotels discreetly allow cannabis use. Always check reviews and house rules, or ask the host directly." [3]

Accommodations in Brno

Brno, the second-largest city in the Czech Republic, is also known for its relaxed atmosphere. Although you won't find as many specialized 420-friendly hotels as in Prague, there are still good options.

  • Airbnb with smoking allowed: The best choice in Brno is to search for apartments and rooms on Airbnb where smoking is permitted. Many hosts are tolerant if you arrange it with them in advance.
  • Hotels near cannabis shops: Accommodation near shops like Hempo Cannabis Shop Brno may indicate a more tolerant environment in the area.

Tips for Finding Accommodation

  1. Filter by "Smoking allowed": On platforms like Airbnb or Booking.com, always use the filter for smoking accommodation.
  2. Read reviews: Previous guests often mention the host's attitude toward cannabis smoking.
  3. Communicate with the host: Don't be afraid to ask in advance about smoking rules. A discreet and polite inquiry can prevent many problems.
  4. Look for accommodation with a balcony or terrace: Even if smoking indoors is not allowed, many places tolerate consumption in outdoor areas.

Important Notice

Possession of small amounts of cannabis for personal use is decriminalized in the Czech Republic, but the sale and distribution of THC are still illegal. Always behave responsibly and respect local laws and accommodation rules. This guide is for informational purposes only.
