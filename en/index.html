Konopí v České Republice

The Czech Republic has a long tradition of cannabis cultivation and in recent years has become one of the most progressive countries in Europe regarding access to cannabis products. CBD products are completely legal in the Czech Republic and available in dozens of specialized stores across the country. Prague's CBD shops offer a wide range of products, from oils and capsules to cosmetics and food enriched with cannabidiol.

Medical cannabis has been legal in the Czech Republic since 2013, and patients with a doctor's prescription can obtain cannabis at pharmacies nationwide. Seed banks and grow shops provide cultivators with everything needed for home growing, including seeds, lighting, fertilizers, and hydroponic systems. The cannabis market in the Czech Republic is continuously expanding, offering increasing opportunities for both consumers and entrepreneurs.
